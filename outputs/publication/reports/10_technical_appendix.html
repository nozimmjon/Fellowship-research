read_csv_safe <- function(path) {
  tryCatch(
    utils::read.csv(path),
    error = function(e) data.frame(note = paste("Could not read", basename(path), "-", conditionMessage(e)))
  )
}

3 C. Identification Strategy

  • Module A fixed measure set: rank-rank slope, transition matrix, upward mobility rate, downward mobility rate, persistence probability
  • Module B: fixed-effects models with household and regional controls
  • Module C: LiTS IV mechanism models (2022-23 only), interpreted as suggestive non-causal evidence
  • Reform/COVID DiD: optional and conditional on validated admin region-time variation
